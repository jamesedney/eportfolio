Who Am I?

Hi, I'm James Edney!

I've been working in IT for around eight years. I started off on a service desk and then worked my way up to leading technical projects and designing network architecture.

I really started getting into my trade during the COVID 19 Pandemic. I was fortunate enough that my work were funding certifications. During this time I passed the CCNA, CCNP enterprise, CompTIA Security+ and a handful of other tests. I'm looking forward to challenging myself with this course.

In my spare time, I'm into running and keeping fit. I also enjoy studying, reading and practicing chess.

Launching into Cybersecurity

Learning outcomes:

  • Identify and explain the architecture, structure and functionality of basic components of a computer system, considering Cyber Security issues.
  • Evaluate critically implications of the key vulnerabilities and threats of software and network security and approaches to mitigate these issues.
  • Appraise critically and apply the concepts and principles of secure object-oriented design to facilitate business security-based decisions.
  • Evaluate critically the solutions developed to solve/mitigate these security issues.
